Our Lab by Week

Select a week from the calendar to see our progress.

June 5 to June 11

Planned protocols and researched experimental design for all the necessary experiments needed throughout the project
Ordered reagents, took stock of the lab to determine which enzymes and reagents we have
Designed DNA fragments of our antibodies to be synthesized

June 12 to June 18

Plasmid backbones arrived, cultured them in preparation for inoculating a liquid DH5α culture
Ran pilot study to determine the doubling time of our DH5α liquid cultures

June 19 to June 25

Synthesized DNA fragments arrived, PCR amplification of fragments
Made competent DH5α cells
Constructed plasmids

June 26 to July 2

PCR amplification of synthesized DNA fragments
Made competent DH5α cells
Constructed plasmids
