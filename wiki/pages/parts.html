Parts Catalog

All of the parts created and documented over the course of the project are on this page. Below is a table of parts added to the registry, along with a plasmid viewer to help visualize all of our parts. All parts seen here have been engineered by the Virginia iGEM Team using components of publicly-available antibody sequences.

Parts Table

We added a total of ten basic parts (BBa_K4477001-BBa_K4477010) and four composite parts (BBa_K4477011-BBa_K4477014) to the registry.
